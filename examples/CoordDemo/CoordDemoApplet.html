CoorDemoApplet:
A Demonstration of the Horizon Coordinate Classes

This applet is a demonstration of a simple viewer that tracks world coordinate positions of data voxels within a dataset. It is provided as an example to programmers on the use of Horizon's coordinate classes. For more information about these classes see the article "Supporting Metadata and Coordinate Systems for Scientific Data in the Horizon Java Package"

If you are reading this, your WWW browser can't or won't do Java(tm). Contact your browser supplier for technical assistance.
Select an image from the menu and then hit "Display" to begin the visualization.
